WELCOME TO KONGU ENGINEERING COLLEGE

Kongu Engineering College was established in 1983 and is located in Erode. The college is AICTE-approved and has private autonomous ownership. Kongu Engineering College is affiliated with Anna University Chennai. The KEC offers seven programmes under five specialisations including Engineering, Science, IT & Software Business & Management Studies, and Design. The top courses include BTech and MBA. It also has five departments including Engineering, Management Studies, Computer Applications, Computer Technology, and Science & Humanities. The college has a committed Trust named ‘The Kongu Vellalar Institute of Technology Trust’ in Erode District, Tamil Nadu. Around 41 philanthropists from the Kongu region collectively formed the Trust ‘The Kongu Vellalar Institute of Technology Trust’ to provide technical education across all sections of society. Nearly 167-acre land area is covered by a KEC campus equipped with infrastructural facilities including a dispensary, WiFi facility, food court, post office, bank, 24/7 power supply, and more.
